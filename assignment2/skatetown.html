Entertainment for the whole family

Skatetown is great venue offering entertainment for the whole family. Whether you are looking for...... somewhere to have a family outing, a place to get the kids out and active, a venue to host your next party or get together, Skatetown has you covered.

Come Roll With Us!

We have regular weekly public sessions offering a full-service snack bar, arcade games, pro-shop, and we must not forget the stuff-shop with all the fun glow in the dark and light-up items as well as candy that the kids are crazy about! We have extended hours when the kids are out on school break and summertime. We host an ALL-NIGHT SKATE event every NEW YEAR'S EVE!
